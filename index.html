About

Twice is a Korean female idol group South formed by JYP Entertainment. The group consists of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. Twice was formed through a television program called Sixteen, and made their debut on the 20th October 2015 with the mini album titled The Story Begins.
